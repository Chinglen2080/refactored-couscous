Working of the Project

When the switch is turned ON and no object is detected, the colorful LED glows, indicating readiness.

When Sensor 1 detects an object within 20 cm, the servo moves 45¬∞, slightly lifting the thermocol road. The orange LED and buzzer turn ON for 8 seconds, showing that a vehicle is approaching.

If the object comes closer (within 15 cm), Sensor 2 activates. The servo then moves 70¬∞, raising the barrier fully. The red and blue LEDs flash alternately, and the buzzer produces an emergency sound.

After 8 seconds, if no object remains, all components turn OFF automatically, and the road barrier returns to its original position.

Mechanical Setup & Real-Life Use

The road is made of thermocol attached to the servo motor horn using glue. The servo is fixed on one side of the base; its rotation lifts the thermocol road just like a real gate. At 45¬∞, it slightly lifts for checking; at 70¬∞, it completely blocks the way.

This system can be used in army camps, toll gates, and security checkpoints to automatically detect and stop vehicles without human effort. The buzzer and lights act as warning indicators, ensuring safety and efficiency.
